Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the most common questions asked by our users. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

The MKU E-Book Store is an online platform that offers a variety of e-books and educational resources to students, lecturers, and staff. It provides a catalog of books that can be accessed, downloaded, and read directly from the platform.
To register, simply click on the "Register" button on the homepage and fill in your details. You’ll need to provide your name, email, password, and choose your role (student, lecturer, or admin). Once submitted, you’ll receive a verification email to complete the registration process.
Yes, you can download e-books from the platform. Once you are logged in, browse the catalog, and for each book, you’ll see a download option available for supported file formats like PDF, EPUB, and MOBI.
Your reading history can be accessed from your user profile. Simply go to the "Profile" section, and there you will see a list of the books you have recently viewed, downloaded, or marked for future reading.
You can provide feedback or report an issue by visiting the "Contact Us" section in the footer of the website. You can fill out a form to send us your comments or report any technical issues you're experiencing.
